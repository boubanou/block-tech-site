Fractional Real Estate: A Democratized Approach to Property Investment 

Fractional real estate breaks down property ownership into smaller, more affordable shares, allowing multiple investors to pool resources and co-own high-value properties. 

 

Benefits of Fractional Ownership with Block Tech 

 

  • Low Entry Thresholds: Invest in properties starting from as little as €10. 
  • Diversification: Spread your investments across multiple properties and regions to reduce risk. 
  • Enhanced Liquidity: Shareholders can sell their stakes on secondary markets, unlike traditional real estate investments. 
  • Scalable Operations: Automate ownership records, rental income distribution, and compliance. 

 

These features make fractional ownership an appealing option for millennials, small-scale investors, and businesses looking to expand their portfolios with minimal capital outlay. 

The Crowdfunding Model: A Win-Win for Investors and Professionals 

Crowdfunding is revolutionizing how real estate projects are funded. By leveraging a community of investors, developers and property owners can secure funding quickly and efficiently. Block Tech supports several crowdfunding models to meet the diverse needs of real estate professionals: 

1. Equity Crowdfunding 

Investors gain partial ownership in the property, entitling them to rental income and capital gains when the property appreciates in value. 

2. Debt Crowdfunding 

This model allows investors to lend money to property developers in exchange for fixed interest rates. It’s ideal for those seeking predictable returns. 

3. Royalties-Based Crowdfunding 

Unique to Block Tech, this model ties investor returns directly to the revenue generated by the property. For example, a share of rental income is distributed regularly, ensuring consistent cash flow for investors. 

Why Royalties-Based Crowdfunding Stands Out 

  • Steady Income: Investors enjoy predictable payouts tied to property performance. 

 

  • Lower Risk: Returns are generated from ongoing revenue, minimizing dependency on market conditions. 

 

  • Regulatory Compliance: Block Tech ensures all transactions align with legal standards, fostering investor confidence.
